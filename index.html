FAQs

What is Veo Cam and how does it help?
Veo Cam is an advanced portable camera system designed specifically for sports recording. It uses AI technology to automatically follow the action during a game without a cameraman. This allows us to capture high-quality footage of the entire match, which we can use to provide detailed video highlights and individual player analysis. By offering visual feedback, it's an invaluable tool for player development, helping to refine skills, understand game dynamics, and improve team performance.

What is Phoenix TV?
Phoenix TV ([https://www.youtube.com/@phoenixgirlsfc) is our club's YouTube channel, a vibrant community hub where we update parents and players about each team's performance. It's not just about the scores; we feature entertaining segments that build our team ethos and togetherness. With footage from the Veo Cam, we can highlight interesting game states, explain football rules, and even run 'spot the ball' and other fun competitions. This interactive content engages players in their own development and brings our football family closer together, celebrating every kick of the ball.
